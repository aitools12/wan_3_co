What is Wan 3?

Wan 3 is an AI-powered video generation platform that turns text prompts and reference images into short-form videos. It supports text-to-video, image-to-video, and prompt-based video editing workflows, making it suitable for creative professionals, content creators, and AI enthusiasts.

Why use Wan 3?

Text-to-Video

Describe your scene and get a generated video in return. Works for storytelling, concepts, and rapid prototyping.

Image-to-Video

Upload a still image and animate it with motion. Keep your visual style while adding movement.

Quick Creation

No video editing skills needed. Just describe or show what you want and get results instantly.

How it works

  1. Input — Write a text prompt or upload a reference image.
  2. Configure — Adjust duration, style, and motion intensity.
  3. Generate — The AI processes your input and produces a downloadable video.

Core use cases

  • Social media content — Quickly generate visuals for posts, ads, and stories.
  • Creative prototyping — Test video ideas before committing to full production.
  • Product demos — Create short demonstration clips for products or services.
  • Artistic exploration — Experiment with AI-generated motion and visual styles.
