## What is Wan 3?

Wan 3 is an AI-powered video generation platform that turns text prompts and reference images into short-form videos. It supports text-to-video, image-to-video, and prompt-based video editing workflows, making it suitable for creative professionals, content creators, and AI enthusiasts who want to produce visual content quickly without traditional video editing tools.

## Why use Wan 3?

Traditional video creation requires editing skills, software licenses, and time. Wan 3 removes these barriers by letting you describe or show what you want and get a generated video in return. It's designed for:

- Rapid prototyping of video ideas
- Social media content creation
- Product demonstration clips
- Creative experimentation with AI-generated motion

## How Wan 3 works

1. **Input**: Write a text prompt or upload a reference image describing the scene you want.
2. **Configure**: Adjust parameters such as duration, style, and motion intensity.
3. **Generate**: The AI processes your input and produces a downloadable video.
